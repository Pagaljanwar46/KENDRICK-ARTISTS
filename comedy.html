Artist Manager/Agent

(AHR-tist MAN-uh-jer)

A person responsible for developing a comedian’s long-term strategy, advising on branding and creative choices, managing relationships with venues, agents, and other industry professionals, and collaborating with booking agents who secure live appearances, negotiate show terms, and coordinate the logistics of performances.
